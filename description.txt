Inspired by LuiAFK, pbone's Utilities adds many combined items to enhance the quality of life, some examples being...
 - An item that automatically sends collected money to your piggy bank.
 - An item to summon a portable safe, similar to the money trough.
 - Bottomless buckets and sponges for lava and honey, as well as a combination item.
 - Magnets to increase your grab range.
 - And item to automatically sell picked up items.
...and more! Details can be found on the mod's homepage.
